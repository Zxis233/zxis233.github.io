图表作文模板

First Paragraph 第一段

Pie Charts 饼图

 Consisting of 板块数量 sections, the pie chart above has clearly shown 翻译图表中的中文短语, in which 占比最高的板块名称 has played a significant role, accounting for 该板块的具体百分比, with 剩余板块名称1, 剩余板块名称2, and 剩余板块名称3 covering 剩余板块名称1的具体百分比, 剩余板块名称2的具体百分比, and 剩余板块名称3的具体百分比 respectively.

开头也可换成 As is shown in the 图表名称, …

 Consisting of 5 sections, the pie chart above has clearly shown the factors consumers pay attention to when choosing a restaurant in one city in 2017, in which specialty has played a significant role, accounting for 36.3%, with service, environment, and price covering 26.8%, 23.8%, and 8.4% respectively.

Bar Charts and Line Charts 柱状图与线图

 What we can see is a bar/line chart about 翻译图表中的中文, in which 上升数据的名称 is on the rapid/moderate rise from 起点数据+年份 to 终点数据+年份, while ①下降数据名称 appears to show a downward trend. //②持平数据名称 has remained almost unchanged. // ③另一组上升数据的名称 has also increased rapidly/moderately/accordingly.

 What we can see is a bar chart about the changes of the population in both rural and urban areas in China over the past 20 years, in which the number of people living in cities is on the rapid rise from 300 million in 1990 to over 600 million in 2010, while the number of those living in countryside appears to show a downward trend.

 What we can see is a line chart about the number of museums and their visitors in our country from 2013 to 2015, in which the number of visitors is on the rapid rise from 637.8 million in 2013 to 781.1 million in 2015, while that of museum has also increased accordingly.

Table 表格

 Clearly shown in this table is 翻译图表中的中文. We can see that 第一列中占比最高的数据名称A take a leading position of 数据名称A在该列的具体数据 in the column of 列1的名称, while the percentage of 该列中占比最低的数据名称B is merely/literally 数据名称B在该列中的具体数据. By contrast, only 数据名称A在列2的具体数据 of the former and up to 数据名称B在列2的具体数据 of the latter marked their answers(sb.)//have been marked (sth.) in the column of 列2的名称.

 Clearly shown in this table is the result of a survey on employees' satisfaction degree toward their jobs in one company. We can see that the people over 50 years old take a leading position of 40% in the column of satisfaction, while the percentage of the people from 41 to 50 years old is literally 0%. By contrast, only 10% of the former and up to 64% of the latter marked their answers in the column of dissatisfaction.

Second Paragraph 第二段

 The following interpretations may offer a clue to have a better understanding of such figures. First, the advance in both economic and culture development of our country has made a growing number of people be more aware of 简化版中文, which is closely related to our everyday life. Second, with all the crucial data included in this 图表类型 , 简化版中文 is also an indicator indispensible when it comes to evaluating the progress we have made to changes lives for the better over the past few years. Third, though rather encouraging, the figures in this 图表类型 will by no means put an end to our striving for the building of an overall well-off society.

 The following interpretations may offer a clue to have a better understanding of such figures. First, the advance in both economic and culture development of our country has made a growing number of people be more aware of the market share of domestic brands, which is closely related to our everyday life. Second, with all the crucial data included in this bar chart, the market share of domestic brands is also an indicator indispensible when it comes to evaluating the progress we have made to changes lives for the better over the past few years. Third, though rather encouraging, the figures in this bar chart will by no means put an end to our striving for the building of an overall well-off society.

Ending Paragraph 结尾段

Positive 积极正面

 In a nutshell, I will personally regard such figures as a manifestation of the efforts we have made in the past few years, which also delivers a message loud and clear ---①our people's living standard has been greatly improved./②the comprehensive national strength of our country has been considerably enhanced.

Negative 消极负面

 To sum up, such figures, in my point of view, can serve as a timely reminder that closer attention has to be paid to 简化版中文, and there is still much room for further improvement. Only through continuous perfection, can we make a difference.

总结
