面试英文介绍模板

发表于2025-03-27|更新于2025-04-02|学习
|字数总计:239|阅读时长:1分钟|阅读量:

Good morning, dear professors. It's my honor to be here for this interview.

My name is Esing. I'm a student from University, majoring in microelectronics.

I am optimistic and willing. I have broad interests from listening to music to making handcraft items about electronics. During the past years, I have my own open-source project relating to e-paper and microcomputer, and my graduate project is 3D printer controller based on embedded system, which runs Klipper on Raspberry Pi Pico. Furthermore, I tried my best in competitions like China College IC Competition and, with my teammates, we get the third prize national.

Although I have broad interests and grasp some essential knowledge of the major, the more I have learned, the more ignorant I feel I am. So, it is important for me to fulfill further study to realize self-value.

As the artificial intelligence become more and more popular, the semiconductor industry has demonstrated its importance. The integrated digital chips are at the forefront of attention, which I have a strong interest in. I plan to concentrate on study and research in this field in my graduate time.

That's all. Thank you for your listening.
